Instructions


As part of the evaluation process, you will be required to complete two rounds. These rounds are designed to assess your skills, knowledge, and suitability for the position you've applied for. During the first round, you will be presented with a series of questions or tasks. These may be in the form of multiple-choice questions, problem-solving exercises, or practical assignments, depending on the nature of the position. Take your time to carefully read and understand each question or task before providing your response. If you successfully pass the first round, you will proceed to the second round. The second round might involve additional assessments, such as a virtual interview, a coding challenge, a presentation, or a simulation exercise. The specific details and requirements for the second round will be communicated to you after the completion of the first round. It's important to approach each round with focus, attention to detail, and a desire to showcase your abilities. Make sure to follow any provided instructions closely and submit your responses within the given timeframe, if applicable. We wish you the best of luck in the upcoming rounds of the pre-interview test. If you have any further questions or require clarification on anything, please don't hesitate to ask.
